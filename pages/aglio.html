Spaghetti Aglio e Olio Recipe

This may look simplsitic, but with the right ingredients, this can be a powerful dish in your culinary arsenal.

INGREDIENTS (serves two):

  • Spaghetti (600g)
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic (2 cloves)
  • Parsley Herbs (as much as you want)
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Chili Flakes
  • Lemon (1/2 slice)

STEPS:

  1. Dice up garlic into tiny bits or blend into a puree
  2. Chop up parsley into tiny bits
  3. Slice lemon in half and squeeze juice into separate bowl
  4. Boil pasta until al dente
  5. On another pan, add 6 Tbsp olive oil. Add garlic. Simmer gently
  6. Once al dente, ad dpasta to pan. Add pasta water. Add parsley
  7. Stir continuously until sauce emulsifies. Add pasta water as needed
  8. Sprinkle some pepper and chili
  9. Serve!!!
