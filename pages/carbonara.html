Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

This is one of my absolue favorite pasta dishes. And it is remarkably simple to make. Let's get right into it!

INGREDIENTS (serves one):

  • Spaghetti (300g)
  • Pecorino Cheese (100g)
  • Parmesan Cheese (100g)
  • Panchetta (100g)
  • Eggs (4x)
  • Black Pepper

STEPS:

  1. Grate parmesan and pecorino cheese to specified weights
  2. Dice up panchetta into small rectangular strips
  3. Boil spaghetti until al dente
  4. As pasta boils, gently cook pancetta on low. Allow fat to melt
  5. Crack open first 3 eggs into bowl, but discard the yolks
  6. Add the 4th egg into the mix; this time with the yolk. Whisk
  7. Add parmesan/pecorno into egg mix. Add some pasta water. Stir well
  8. Once al dente, remove spaghetti and place on pan with pancetta
  9. Add some pasta water and stir spaghetti with panchetta and fat
  10. Turn off stove and add cheese/egg mix onto pasta. Stir until creamy
  11. Sprinkle some pepper and stir
  12. Serve !!!
